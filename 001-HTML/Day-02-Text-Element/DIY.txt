#  *INTERVIEW QUESTIONS*

Q. What is the difference between <b> and <strong>?
A. Both <b> and <strong appears to be make the text bold but they serve differnt purpose.
   The <b> tag is purely visual, whereas <strong> adds semantic importance to the text, 
   which helps search engines and screen readers understand its meaning.

Q. What is the difference between <i> and <em>?
A. The <i> tag in HTML is used to display text in italics. It is generally used for stylistic purposes, 
   such as denoting a different mood, voice, or foreign word, without adding any semantic 
   emphasis to the content.
